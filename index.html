Welcome to MARBLE PUZZLE GAME

The Rules for The Marble Puzzle are as follows -

  1. The player makes successive capturing moves, removing a single piece each turn until is it impossible to make any more capturing moves.
  2. Each turn, the player captures a piece by jumping over that piece orthogonally (not diagonally) from one adjacent point to the vacant adjacent point on the other side.
  3. Therefore, the first turn can be made only by jumping a piece into the middle hole from one of 4 possible points.
